Quantum Billiards Simulation Videos

The following videos are supplementary material to the Quantum Billiards Master's Thesis by Callum Brown. Please note that some of the videos for large heavy ions have a drop in frame rate due to them being produced on a relatively old laptop - this is NOT a real physical phenomenon.

Please note also that there have been issues rendering animations for the Lab Frame and so particles have glitched - either not moving or not producing products. Work is currently being done to fix this.
